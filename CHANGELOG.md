# Version 3.2.0 — Personalized Detective Edition

- Removed the fixed Detective Lena Cross identity from the player experience.
- Solo players are now displayed and addressed as `Detective [chosen name or alias]`.
- Team games are displayed as `[team name] Detective Team` and addressed collectively as `Detectives of [team name]`.
- Personalized all six chapter briefings in every hidden case version.
- Personalized the top navigation identity, briefing badge initials, accusation confirmation, and final reconstruction.
- Kept all personalization server-generated so the correct wording follows the player across devices and resumed sessions.
- Updated the service-worker cache to force delivery of the new interface.
- Added automated solo and team personalization tests.
