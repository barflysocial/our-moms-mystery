# Personalized Detective Upgrade Report

## Solo play
A player who chooses the alias `Ray` is shown as **Detective Ray**. Briefings begin with wording such as:

> Detective Ray, your next briefing follows.

## Team play
A table named `Bayou Sleuths` is shown as **Bayou Sleuths Detective Team**. Briefings begin with:

> Detectives of Bayou Sleuths, your next briefing follows.

## Updated surfaces
- Top app identity
- Chapter briefing speaker and badge initials
- Audio narration transcript
- Briefing assignment heading
- Final accusation confirmation
- Final reconstruction heading and narration

## Security and continuity
The server personalizes only the currently available briefing and final reveal. Hidden case versions and solutions remain protected. The chosen identity is restored when the player resumes on another device.
